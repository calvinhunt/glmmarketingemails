First, sometime this week, go and listen to my interview with David Vicknair of Scott Vicknair, LLC., on the Renegade Lawyer Podcast.

David is building a major force in the New Orleans personal injury market, a highly competitive market.

 

Here are some big ideas from his episode.

 

1. Geographic Expansion and Office Strategy: The firm expanded geographically, opening two appointment-only offices in addition to their main offices. This strategy of starting with unstaffed, appointment-only offices in new locations is a low-risk way to increase presence and client base used by many of our top GLM members. It can be a real Internet play for you.

 

2. Marketing and Client Relationships: The firm places a strong emphasis on referral marketing, maintaining a client newsletter, and engaging in unique gestures like sending personalized gratitude books to clients. Too many lawyers are looking at the next bright, shiny object without having perfected the basics.

 

3. Leveraging Technology and Outsourcing: The firm uses tools like Slack for internal communication and leverages outsourcing for certain functions.  Small firms can adopt similar technologies to streamline operations and reduce overhead and I am constantly surprised when I attend "normal" lawyer events and hear how few lawyers understand, let alone, maximize the technology tools that our members take for granted.

 

4. Focus on Culture and Employee Development: The firm emphasizes a positive work culture and invests constantly in employee development. Again, in talking to a lot of young lawyers, in particular, I'm shocked at how few firms see the value in investing in the development of their associates.

 

5. Adopting a Business Mindset: David talks about learning from mistakes, focusing on management and projections, and the freedom to choose what to do with time. This reflects the shift from just practicing law to running a law practice as a business. For GLM members, adopting a business mindset – focusing on growth, strategy, and efficient management – is key to scaling up to seven figures.
