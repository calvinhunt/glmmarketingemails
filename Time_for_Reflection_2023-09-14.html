In her book, Dare to Lead, Brene Brown reminds us:

Live a Life of Gratitude – when you are working with groups of people, it is our responsibility to say to the people who work alongside us, "We've got to stop and celebrate one another and our victories, no matter how small – yes, we could hit bumps in the road, but we need to celebrate our accomplishments right now".

2023 has been an amazing year for Brian and I and I hope you can say the same about your own life.

 

When I say that I'm not just talking about money, or cases, or even time.

 

I'm talking about LIFE itself.

 

It where the whole Live Life Big thing, that permeates much of our local branding for the law firm, comes from.

 

Life.

 

My wish for you is that you find some space in your life in the next week to think back and reflect on your victories so far this year. What challenges have your overcome? What goals have you met?

 

Not there yet? That's OK, too, as long as you can clearly identify a goal, have a plan and are honestly executing on the plan, you will get there, I promise. 

 

Again:

  1. Identify Goal
  2. Create Plan
  3. Execute on Plan

Find some time to sit in peace, paper journal and pen in hand, and just think about what you have accomplished. Think about yourself first. Give yourself the gift of this type of celebration. 

 

You are heroic. You get up every morning with a purpose. That purpose you have set out by deliberate design. You know that no purpose is achieved without work.
