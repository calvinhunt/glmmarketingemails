This Sunday afternoon our leadership team at the law firm heads out to a a rental house in the wood for our annual two day retreat. That's six on leadership, plus our coach, huddled together in a pretty nice house, away from the hustle of the office, to dig in.

 

We go backwards first, closely examining 2023, the goals we set for it and whether we hit them or not. (Spoiler alert: we did not hit all of our goals.)

 

When you don't hit goal there are two basic questions to ask:

 

  1. did we set the right goals?
  2. If yes, then what happened?

Our 2023 goals were very aggressive. At the same time, we change four different firm technologies (website vendor and platform change, added Lead Docket, move or LTD cases to CasePeer from Practice Panther, and changed out our virtual answering team.

 

Most of that worked well. The web process is frustratingly slow (though 80% of our dollars come from humans, not digital marketing) and this year we are moving our intake / sales team in-house for everything except after hours.

 

Three people left, so there was change there as well.

 

Here's what we'll dig into at the house in the woods:

 

What do we want out of the practice? 

 

Three things come top of mind:

 

  1. healthy compensation,
  2. a great lifestyle and
  3. a low risk of catastrophic failure.

 

     The purpose of our annual retreat is to:

 

     1.   Make the vision clear and keep team health high.

 

     2.   Put the blocks in place to achieve the vision.

 

     We also look at what new opportunities are out there. So much is changing, especially in the digital marketing world that we we all seem to fret over.

 

We'll be reporting on this more in depth to our two in-person mastermind groups who will each be coming in here later this month. We've got some folks new to the groups and we are excited about digging into their practice as well.

 

I'll leave you with one cool new thing we are working on and that is the integration our one of our oral argument podcasts with the creation of new blog/article content at the website.

 

Now that I've taught my out of country VA how to fetch more of the oral argument raw material and set up new podcast episodes, I don't have to touch that process much.

 

Go check it out.

 

Have a great weekend. We are excited about ours!
